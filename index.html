tour program

  • Arrive in Auckland. Spend the day in the country's largest city: visit the Auckland Tower for panoramic views of the city, stroll along the Viaduct Promenade, and in the evening, sample local cuisine and wines at the restaurants of Wynyard Road.

  • Head to Rotorua (approximately 3 hours drive), known for its geothermal springs and Maori culture. Visit Wai-O-Tapu Geothermal Park with geysers and colorful lakes, and Te Puia Maori Park for a glimpse of Haki traditions and dances.

  • Early departure to Tongariro National Park - New Zealand's first national park, known for its active volcanoes. A walk along the Tongariro Alpine Crossing route will allow you to see the volcanoes Ngauruhoe and Ruapehu, picturesque emerald lakes and unforgettable views.
